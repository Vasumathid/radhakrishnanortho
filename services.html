01 / Joint Replacement Surgery

Knee & Hip Replacement, with same-day mobilisation

When arthritis or injury has worn down a joint beyond what medication and physiotherapy can manage, joint replacement restores both function and quality of life — and modern minimally invasive technique means recovery starts almost immediately.

Total & partial knee replacementFull or single-compartment resurfacing depending on the extent of joint damage.
Hip arthroplastyReplacing a damaged hip joint with a prosthesis to eliminate pain and restore mobility.
Revision (re-do) joint replacementFor patients whose earlier implant needs to be revised or replaced.
Shoulder replacementFor severe shoulder arthritis or joint damage affecting arm mobility.
You may be a candidate if: walking, climbing stairs, or simply standing from a chair has become consistently painful, and conservative treatment (medication, physiotherapy, injections) is no longer giving lasting relief.

02 / Sports Medicine

Ligament & tendon care to get you back in play

Whether it's a weekend football injury or a professional athlete's ligament tear, sports injuries need fast, accurate diagnosis and a recovery plan built around getting back to the activity you love — not just resting it away.

ACL & PCL reconstructionArthroscopic ligament reconstruction for knee stability after a tear.
Ligament & tendon repairSurgical and non-surgical repair of torn or strained ligaments and tendons.
Knee & shoulder arthroscopyMinimally invasive keyhole surgery to diagnose and treat joint injuries.
Shoulder dislocation treatmentReduction and stabilisation for recurring or acute shoulder dislocations.
You may be a candidate if: you've experienced a sudden twist, pop, or impact during sport or exercise, followed by swelling, instability, or an inability to bear weight on the joint.
Get Your Injury Assessed →
03 / Spine & Neck Care

Back and neck pain, treated without rushing to surgery

Chronic back pain and slipped discs are some of the most over-treated conditions in orthopaedics. Dr. Radhakrishnan's approach starts with a precise diagnosis of what's actually causing the pain — disc, nerve, muscle, or posture — before deciding what's needed.

Slipped (herniated) disc careConservative and surgical management of spinal disc disorders.
Neck pain & cervical spondylosisTreatment for stiffness, pinched nerves and degenerative neck conditions.
Chronic pain managementLong-term care plans for ongoing back and joint pain, beyond a single visit.
Joint dislocation treatmentRealignment and stabilisation of dislocated joints with follow-up care.
You may be a candidate if: back or neck pain has lasted more than a few weeks, radiates into an arm or leg, or has already led another doctor to suggest surgery you'd like a second opinion on.
Get a Spine Assessment →
04 / Arthritis Management

Slowing the damage before it needs surgery

Arthritis doesn't have one fixed treatment path. Early-stage osteoarthritis can often be managed for years through targeted medication, joint injections and the right exercise plan — preserving the joint rather than replacing it.

Osteoarthritis managementThe most common form of arthritis — managed through medication and lifestyle guidance.
Joint injectionsTargeted injections to reduce inflammation and ease joint pain directly.
Osteoporosis screening & careDiagnosis and prevention of bone density loss to reduce fracture risk.
Guided exercise & physiotherapyMovement plans designed to protect joints while keeping you active.
You may be a candidate if: you notice morning stiffness, swelling, or joint pain that worsens with activity — especially in the knees, hips, or hands — and want to manage it before it progresses.
Start Managing Your Arthritis →
05 / Limb Deformity Correction

Correcting alignment, restoring confidence

Bowed legs, length discrepancies, or deformities present from birth or caused by old fractures can affect both movement and self-confidence. Modern correction techniques realign limbs with precision and a clear functional goal.

Limb alignment correctionSurgical correction of bowed or misaligned limbs in adults and children.
Pediatric orthopaedic careSpecialised assessment and treatment for growth-related deformities in children.
Post-fracture deformity correctionAddressing alignment issues left behind from improperly healed fractures.
You may be a candidate if: a limb appears visibly bowed, uneven in length, or has healed crooked after a past injury, affecting gait or comfort.
Discuss Correction Options →
06 / Trauma & Fracture Care

When it can't wait — urgent fracture care

Road accidents, falls, and sudden impacts need fast, accurate fracture management. International Ortho Care provides full trauma assessment with on-site digital X-ray, so a fracture can be diagnosed and stabilised in the same visit.

Fracture fixationSurgical and non-surgical stabilisation of broken bones, from simple casts to internal fixation.
Emergency trauma assessmentRapid evaluation of road traffic accident and fall injuries with on-site imaging.
Hand & finger surgerySpecialised repair for hand fractures, tendon injuries, and finger replantation cases.
Seek care immediately if: there's visible deformity, inability to move or bear weight on a limb, or significant swelling and pain following any fall or accident.
